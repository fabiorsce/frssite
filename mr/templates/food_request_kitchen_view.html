Express Food Request

You are in a express restaurant. You can see the menu online and make your own requests.

After choose the food and drinks and make a request, you will go to the cashier to pay for your request.

The cashier can see all the requests. He can cancel a request or receive the payment and change the request to paid.

The kitchen can see all the paid requests. They can start working in a request changing the status to PREPARING. When the request is finished, the kitchen will change the status to DONE

The client can follow all the progress of his request to savor when it is done.

You can see all the views right below.
